Research experience

Research assistant

2021.03-present /Research group on micro/nano device reliability
Instructor: Li yuan - Associate Professor of Shandong University
1. The first organic TFET based on two dimensional covalent-organic frameworks (2D COFs) with high Ion (caused by van del waals hetrojunction) and dopingless characteristic (caused by fermi level movement from bilayer COFs in electrode). MetalloPhthalocyanine (MPc) COFs used in this article realize high-ordered system and tunable characteristic (compared to disordered organic systems such as polymers or small molecules). Bilayer MPc in electrode, compared with monolayer MPc in channel, realized fermi level movement like doping and make bandgap smaller to narrower on-state tunneling barrier.
2. High Ion WTe2 TFET (come from surface state in channel/electrode interface) of 2229.85 μA/μm, which can meet the Ion goals of International Roadmap for Device and Systems (IRDS) for high performance (HP) devices. We introduce a surface state gap (SSG) bonded by van del waals bonds in channel/electrode interface. SSG can introduce surface state in on-state tunneling barrier to assist tunneling. Compared to normal trap-assist tunneling (TAT) that assist tunneling in a little energy. Surface state assist tunneling in almost all the bias window and get 1-2 orders of magnitude higher Ion than TAT TFET.
